About Me

Ruth Van Gurp is a graphic designer and interdisciplinary artist based in Ontario, Canada, between Hamilton and the Niagara Region. Ruth has accrued over ten years of cumulative professional experience in graphic design and product photography, primarily in the agricultural industry. She is also a gifted fine artist, specializing in traditional media such as acrylic and oil painting, and photography.
